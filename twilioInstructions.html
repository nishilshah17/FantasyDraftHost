Twilio Setup Instructions

Twilio is the service that allows this website to receive your texts

  • Sign up for a free account at twilio.com/try-twilio
  • Select "Get your Twilio number" on the new screen and copy this number into the Twilio settings form
  • Click "Choose this number"
  • Click on your email address in the top right of the page and select "Account" in the drop down menu
  • Scroll down to the section labeled "API Credentials"
  • Copy the AccountSID on the LEFT side into the Twilio settings form
  • Click on the lock under AuthToken and copy the token into the Twilio settings form
  • Congrats! You're done. Save your settings, and your league is ready to draft.
